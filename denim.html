    Sewing
    Denim

    1. Denim frays and can be very hard to cut through particularly when layered. Use sharp cutting tools. Start your project off right by using sharp tools to cut your prepped fabric. 2. This fabric is built to be durable and can bend or break your sewing machine needle. Use a denim needle, which is a strong thick needle with a very sharp point. Itâ€™s made to handle the challenge of sewing denim 3. Use the right machine accessories made for denim sewing. Choose the foot and settings on your machine that are best suited to getting through thick fabrics. 4. Reduce bulk. Miter corners and press frequently to minimize bulk and keep seams smooth. 5. Sew slowly. Rushing is bad. Slowly is sexier and good for denim sewing here. Firmly hold your fabric on either side of the presser foot without pushing or pulling.
